About Us

Welcome to Sunnyside Dairy, where quality and freshness come together in every product. We are dedicated to bringing you the finest dairy products from well-known brands such as Milky Mist, Aavin, and Amul. Whether you're looking for fresh milk, delicious sweets, or indulgent ice creams, we've got something for everyone!

Our Story

Founded with the mission to deliver high-quality dairy products directly to your doorstep, Sunnyside Dairy has become a trusted name for families across the region. Our commitment to freshness and sustainability is at the heart of everything we do. We work closely with local dairy farms to ensure that every product is not only fresh but also responsibly sourced.

Our Values

  • Freshness: We ensure that all products are delivered fresh to your door, every time.
  • Sustainability: We partner with eco-conscious farmers to support sustainable practices.
  • Quality: Our products are rigorously tested to meet the highest quality standards.
  • Customer Satisfaction: We prioritize customer satisfaction above all else, ensuring your experience with us is always positive.

Our Commitment

At Sunnyside Dairy, we are not just about providing dairy products; we are about creating a lifestyle centered around health, freshness, and sustainability. We aim to build long-lasting relationships with our customers, ensuring that every product you purchase from us contributes to a healthier and happier life.
